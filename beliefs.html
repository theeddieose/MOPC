Our Beliefs and Tenets

Our beliefs are the building blocks of our faith and movement and are deeply rooted in the Bible and revelations from God.

The Holy Trinity

God is three distinct individuals - God, the Father, God, the Son and God, the Holy Spirit - in one true God.

1 Corinthians 8: 6, John 10: 30, 1 John 5: 7-8, John 14: 9-11, Colossians 2:9, 2 Corinthians 3: 17

Jesus' death and resurrection

We strongly believe that Jesus came to the earth for the purpose of reconciling man with God and did so by paying the price for our sins through his death and restoring victory over sin to man through his resurrection.

Mark 8: 31, Luke 23: 44-47, John 19: 29-30, Mark 16: 5-7, Matthew 28: 18-20

There is salvation for the lost

With the death and resurrection of Jesus, came the ultimate gateway by which man could go to God. For as many that would come out of sin and seek God in repentance, through Jesus Christ, there is the gift of redemption.

Ephesians 2: 8-9, 2 Corinthians 5: 21, Romans 10: 9-10, Hebrews 7:25

Love is the greatest of all

The greatest instruction Jesus ever gave us was to love. Love God, love yourself and love everyone around you and let love guide every of your actions. We strive to live by this always.

1 Corinthians 13: 1-8, Romans 12: 9-10, Mark 12: 29-31, 1 Corinthians 13: 13

Purity of heart

As children of God, it is paramount that we live our lives daily in holiness and strict adherence with the word of God, guiding our hearts with the righteousness of God.

Proverbs 21: 21, Psalm 37: 28, Matthew 6: 33, Proverbs 21: 3, Psalms 1: 1

Rapture

We strongly believe in the second coming of Jesus and we daily admonish ourselves to prepare to spend eternity with our Creator in heavenly bliss. Heaven and hell are real, and so is the judgment, hence the need to live righteously on earth.

2 Corinthians 5: 10, Mark 13: 32, Matthew 24: 30-31, Daniel 12: 1-2, 1 Corinthians 15: 51-53

Grace

Without the grace of God, we know we are nothing. We operate solely by the grace of God that gives us ability to do great exploits and even our salvation is hinged on it. Great Grace is our watchword, because we are testimonies of God's unending grace.

2 Corinthians 12: 9, Ephesians 4: 7, Ephesians 2: 8-9, Titus 2: 11, Romans 11: 6

The Anointing Oil

We believe in the power of God deposited in the anointing oil for breakthrough and deliverance, hence we generously use it in prayer and making supplications unto God.

James 5: 14, Mark 6: 13, Psalm 45: 7, Matthew 6: 17

Tithing and Giving

Giving tithes, offerings and alms to the poor and needy are instructions that God laid down for us to live by. It is compulsory that we give one-tenth of our earnings to the house of God, and also spare no resources in assisting our brothers and sisters. This gladdens God's heart.

Luke 6: 38, Proverbs 11: 24-25, Malachi 3: 8-12, Matthew 6: 1-4, Deuteronomy 14: 22
