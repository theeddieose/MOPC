We Are Perfection!

We Love God. We Love People. We Love You!


We're a generation of God-lovers raised as disciples with godly discipline, dedicated to serving Jesus and humanity, and changing the world through the preaching of the gospel and demonstration of love to all people and nations.

Our Mission

"- for the Son of Man has come to seek and to save that which was lost."John 19 vs 10 (NKJV)

Guided by the Word of God, we have been sent into an unwavering commitment to teaching and preaching of God's undiluted word, for the total salvation and transformation of lives and nations.

Our Vision

"- for the Son of Man has come to seek and to save that which was lost."John 19 vs 10 (NKJV)

Based on this scripture, we operate with a three-tier vision commisioned to our Bishop:

  • Go and perfect my people.
  • Go and teach my people that they are spirit.
  • Go and prepare my people for rapture.

The Goal

"I press toward the goal for the prize of the upward call of God in Christ Jesus."Philippians 3 vs 14 (NKJV)

No vision can be achieved without a goal and so, by the inspiration of the Holy Spirit, we run with two clear goals

  • To prepare men and women for eternity with Christ Jesus.
  • To raise godly people, families and societies in every nation of the earth as Christ's ambassadors
